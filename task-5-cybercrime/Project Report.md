While researching phishing, what surprised me most was how easily attackers can create fake websites and messages that look almost identical to real ones. It is alarming that even a small mistake, like clicking on a wrong link, can lead to serious consequences such as financial loss or identity theft. I also learned that phishing attacks often rely more on human error than technical weaknesses.

As a result, one habit I will personally change is being more cautious before clicking on any link or sharing information online. I will always check the authenticity of emails, messages, and websites before taking any action. Additionally, I will enable two-factor authentication on all my important accounts. This awareness will help me stay safer online and avoid becoming a victim of cybercrime.
