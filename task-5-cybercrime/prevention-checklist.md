# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Never share your passwords, OTPs, or personal details with anyone
- Always verify links before clicking on them
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money (it is only for sending money)
- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” in UPI apps
- Double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment

## 📢 Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report incidents at: National Cyber Crime Portal – https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for immediate assistance)
