# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before posting anything online
- Use privacy settings to protect personal information
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain respectful and polite communication
- Build a positive digital footprint

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful content
- Do not spread fake news or unverified information
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or trolling
- Do not misuse others' photos or content without permission
